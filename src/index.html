About Peg It! - Your Smart Washing Line Weather App

Peg It! is the UK's smartest weather app for hanging washing outside. Using real-time weather data, we calculate the perfect times to dry clothes outdoors based on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rain probability.

Why Use Peg It! for Your Laundry?

  • Save Energy: Reduce tumble dryer usage by up to 80% with perfect outdoor drying timing
  • Fabric-Specific Forecasts: Get accurate drying times for cotton, heavy items, delicates, and bedding
  • Rain Alerts: Know exactly when to bring washing in before it gets wet
  • 5-Day Planning: Plan your laundry week ahead with extended forecasts
  • UK Weather Data: Accurate forecasts for cities across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best weather for drying clothes outside?

The ideal conditions are: 15-25¬∞C temperature, humidity below 50%, gentle breeze of 10-25 km/h, and less than 5% chance of rain. Peg It! calculates these conditions automatically for you.

Absolutely! Peg It! is completely free and requires no registration. Just enter your location and start getting smart washing forecasts.
